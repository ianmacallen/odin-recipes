Lasagna

A favorite dish at Mama Leone's

Sauce for Filling

  • 1/2 lb beef
  • 1/2 lb pork
  • parsley
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • olive oil
  • 4 oz butter
  • 2 oz pancetta
  • 3/4 lb onions
  • salt
  • pepper
  • 24 oz tomatos
  • 1 green pepper
  • chopped meatballs
  • 3 oz toamto paste
  • Brown pancetta in butter and oil.
  • Add onions, garlic and parrsley
  • Add ground meat
  • Salt and pepper
  • Add tomatoes
  • Add green pepper
  • Simmer for abotu 20 minutes
  • Add meat balls
  • Simmer for 90 minutes
  • Add tomato paste and stir.

Sauce for Topping

  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons cream
  • 1 cup toamto sauce
  • 2 tablespoons flour
  • Combine butter and cream
  • Heat gently
  • Add tomatoes
  • Stir in flour

Lasagna

  • Filling sauce
  • Topping sauce
  • Cooked lasagna noodles
  • 2 lbs ricotta
  • Meat Balls
  • 2 cups Parmesan
  • Add layer of filling s auce
  • Layer in three noodles
  • Spread 1 lb ricotta
  • Add some meat balls
  • Add some Parmesan
  • Add sauce and repeat layer
  • Cover with a layer of noodles
  • Spoon tooping sauce over noodles
  • Cover and bake for 20 minutes at 350f
