Sauce for Filling

Ingredients

  • 1/2 lb beef
  • 1/2 lb pork
  • parsley
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • olive oil
  • 4 oz butter
  • 2 oz pancetta
  • 3/4 lb onions
  • salt
  • pepper
  • 24 oz tomatos
  • 1 green pepper
  • chopped meatballs
  • 3 oz toamto paste

Instructions

  1. Brown pancetta in butter and oil.
  2. Add onions, garlic and parrsley
  3. Add ground meat
  4. Salt and pepper
  5. Add tomatoes
  6. Add green pepper
  7. Simmer for abotu 20 minutes
  8. Add meat balls
  9. Simmer for 90 minutes
  10. Add tomato paste and stir.

Sauce for Topping

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons cream
  • 1 cup toamto sauce
  • 2 tablespoons flour

Instructions

  1. Combine butter and cream
  2. Heat gently
  3. Add tomatoes
  4. Stir in flour

Lasagna

Ingredients

  • Filling sauce
  • Topping sauce
  • Cooked lasagna noodles
  • 2 lbs ricotta
  • Meat Balls
  • 2 cups Parmesan

Instructions

  1. Add layer of filling s auce
  2. Layer in three noodles
  3. Spread 1 lb ricotta
  4. Add some meat balls
  5. Add some Parmesan
  6. Add sauce and repeat layer
  7. Cover with a layer of noodles
  8. Spoon tooping sauce over noodles
  9. Cover and bake for 20 minutes at 350f
