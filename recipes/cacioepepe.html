Cacio e Pepe

A historic recipe that reappeared in the 2000s

  • Pasta
  • Finely grated romano cheese
  • Finely ground black pepper
  • Cook pasta
  • Reserve 2 cups of cooking water
  • Drain pasta
  • Add a small amount of water
  • Stir in cheese
  • Add a small amount of water to smooth out
  • Add pepper
  • Stir
