Fettuccine Alfredo

This simple pasta was created around 1909

For an authentic experience, the pasta should be stirred with oversized, golden utensils.

  • Rarmigiano reggiano
  • Butter
  • Butter
  • Butter
  • Egg Pasta

Instructions

  1. Boil Pasta
  2. Reserve water and drain
  3. Stir pasta with butter
  4. Stir in finely grated cheese
  5. Add a splash of pasta water
  6. Add more butter
  7. Toss in a bit more cheese
  8. Top with butter and serve
